Athletic Trainer Opportunities with Cognito Systems

Overview

Cognito Systems, a Planned Systems International (PSI) joint venture, is seeking qualified Athletic Trainers to support vital military healthcare programs across the United States and select international locations. These positions offer unique opportunities to make a meaningful impact on the health and readiness of our nation's service members through two innovative programs

Marine Corps' Sports Medicine Injury Prevention (SMIP) Program - A specialized initiative focused on maintaining and enhancing Marine performance and readiness through expert athletic training care

Army's Holistic Health and Fitness (H2F) System - A comprehensive program focused on optimizing soldier readiness and performance through preventative care,

Minimum Qualifications

Education: Bachelor's Degree in Athletic Training or similar degree from a college or university accredited by the Commission on Accreditation of Athletic Training Education (CAATE)

Certification: Passing score from the BOC for the Athletic Trainer; National Provider Identifier (NPI)

Licensure: Unrestricted state license/registration/certification in any of the 49 regulated states, the District of Columbia, or the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, Guam, or the US Virgin Islands to provide healthcare within the scope of practice for Athletic Trainers per state practice acts

Experience:
• H2F Program: Two years of relevant Athletic Training work experience within the last three years
‍
• SMIP Program: Five years of relevant Athletic Training work experience

Population Experience: Experience working with active populations (e.g., military, high school, collegiate, professional sports, performing arts, occupational health)

Citizenship: U.S. Citizenship

Credentialing: Ability to pass background check and drug screen; current physical and immunizations

Physical Requirements: Possess and maintain the ability to lift and manipulate loads or weights up to 20 kilograms/45lbs
