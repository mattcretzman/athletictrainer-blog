Athletic Trainers:
Your Skills, Their Mission

A Career Opportunity Unlike Any Other in Athletic Training

At Cognito Systems, a Planned Systems International (PSI) joint venture, we're recruiting athletic trainers at all career stages for exceptional roles supporting America's military. Whether you're a recent graduate or an experienced professional, this opportunity allows you to apply your athletic training expertise in a unique setting where your work directly impacts service member health and military readiness.

Discover Open Positions ➜

Meaningful Impact:
Athletic Training That Makes a Difference

In the United States Marine Corps' Sports Medicine Injury Prevention (SMIP) Program or Army's Holistic Health and Fitness (H2F) System, you'll apply your athletic training skills to optimize performance and prevent injuries for elite military personnel. This isn't just another athletic training job—it's a chance to contribute to something bigger.

"I never imagined I could use my athletic training skills in such a meaningful way. Every day, I see how my work directly helps service members perform better and stay injury-free."

— Current SMIP Athletic Trainer

Key Points :

Apply your athletic training education to real-world challenges that matter nationally

Work with elite tactical athletes who depend on your expertise for mission readiness

Implement prevention strategies that protect service members from career-threatening injuries

Experience direct results of your interventions on performance and recovery

Become part of a groundbreaking approach to military healthcare and human performance
